# RXN Sports - Brand Research & Design Direction

## Brand Overview

- **Company**: RXN Sports
- **Founded**: 1972 (Manufacturing Excellence Since 1972)
- **Focus**: Weightlifting, Boxing, MMA, Wrestling, Fitness, Taekwondo, and Badminton
- **Motto**: "Weapon to Win"
- **Positioning**: Trusted by athletes across India, original products built for performance and durability
- **Key Messaging**: "From raw material to fight-ready gear - Crafted in Bharat, played worldwide"

## Current Website Analysis

- **Logo**: Red and black shield design with "RXN" branding
- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary: Red (#E63946 or similar) - Bold, energetic, associated with power
  - Secondary: Black - Professional, strong, contrasts with red
  - Accent: White - Clean, crisp
- **Typography**: Modern, sans-serif (appears to be clean, readable)
- **Product Categories**:
  - Boxing (gloves, punching bags, protective gear, headgear, shoes)
  - Weightlifting (shoes, belts, sleeves, straps, supports)
  - Fitness, Wrestling, MMA, Taekwondo, Badminton
- **Tone**: Professional, performance-focused, athlete-centric

## Design Philosophy for New Website

**Aesthetic**: Bold, Modern, High-Energy Sports Brand

- **Core Principle**: "Weapon to Win" - Aggressive yet refined, powerful yet accessible
- **Visual Language**: Contrast, strength, precision, motion
- **Target Audience**: Serious athletes, fitness enthusiasts, competitive fighters

## Design Direction Chosen

**"Aggressive Minimalism with Bold Accents"**

- Clean, modern layouts with strategic use of red and black
- High-contrast typography for impact
- Minimal but purposeful design elements
- Strong CTAs with red accents
- Hero sections with athletic imagery
- Motion and energy through animations
- Product showcases with emphasis on quality and performance

## Key Features to Implement

1. **Hero Section**: Bold, high-energy with athletic imagery
2. **Product Categories**: Clean grid layout with category cards
3. **Brand Story**: Emphasis on heritage (since 1972) and quality
4. **Contact Form**: Professional, minimal design
